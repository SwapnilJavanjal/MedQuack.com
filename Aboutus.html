About Us

The MedQuack HealthCare System is open to all patients every day and provides fundamental medical care and cutting-edge medicine in a central location in New Jersey. We use our superior academic knowledge to treat a wide range of health issues, taking a personal touch and utilizing highly specialized and up-to-date research.

  • Each year, we treat around 38,000 inpatients and 134,000 outpatients at our 43 departments and institutes
  • Patients with complex and multiple health problems travel from all over the country and abroad to be treated here
  • More than 8,500 employees from 86 countries work at the University Hospital Zurich

Quality

Our clinics, centers and institutes are known at an international level for their achievements in medicine, nursing, research and education. The MedQuack HealthCare Services utilizes highly specialized medicine to successfully treat even the most complex diseases. Our primary objective is to provide the best therapy with the best possible quality of care for every patient.The priority quality targets are efficiency, effectiveness, safety and focus on the patient. The quality report was developed at the University Hospital and is based on the requirements of the H+qualité label.
